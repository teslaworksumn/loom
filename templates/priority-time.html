Scheduled Priority Time

Priority time was invented under the premise that even when a group really needs to use the Labs at a specific time, they likely won't need an entire room or every machine.

Therefore, when a group has priority time they have not made a reservation - instead, they are only given the authority to request immediate use of a work spot or machine they need.

With priority time, you can:

  • Ask to use a machine someone is working on if you need it
  • Ask someone to evacuate a space you need to work in

With priority time, you cannot:

  • Ask to use a machine someone is working on, but use it non-productively (Facebook)
  • "Reserve" the entire room. Other people are allowed to fill the face

This system only works if priority time is the exception, not something that is reserved regularly or defensively. Therefore, reserve as little priority time as you can and only with a specific and good reason.

To reserve priority time in the Labs, contact your group's officers. See you around!

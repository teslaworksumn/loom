Scheduled Priority Time

Priority time was invented under the premise that even when a group really needs to use the Labs at a specific time, they likely won't need an entire room or every machine.

Therefore, when a group has priority time they have not made a reservation - instead, they are only given the authority to request immediate use of a work spot or machine they need.

However, with priority time, you may not:

  • Ask to use a machine someone is working on, but use it non-productively (Facebook)
  • "Reserve" the entire room; Other people are allowed to fill the space your group does not use

This system only works if priority time is the exception, not something that is reserved regularly or defensively. Therefore, reserve as little priority time as you can and only with a specific and good reason. A readable project name ("Sam Smo's Stupendous Singing Serenader" vs "SSSSS" or "Tesla Works") and contact email are also required, or your prioirty time is invalidated.

To reserve priority time in the Labs, contact your group's officers. Thanks all, and see you around!
